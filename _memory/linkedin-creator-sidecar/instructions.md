# LinkedIn Creator Instructions

## Command Execution Guidelines

### [LP] LinkedIn Post Creation
1. Understand the topic/message
2. Structure the post:
   - Hook (first 2 lines - visible before "see more")
   - Body (value, insights, story)
   - Call to engagement (question or action)
3. Format guidelines:
   - Short paragraphs (1-2 lines)
   - Use line breaks for readability
   - Strategic emoji placement (not excessive)
   - No external links in main post
4. Provide 2-3 variations

### [LS] Storytelling Post
1. Identify the story angle:
   - Personal experience
   - Client success
   - Lesson learned
   - Behind-the-scenes
2. Structure:
   - Hook: Unexpected opening or result
   - Setup: Context (brief)
   - Conflict/Challenge: What happened
   - Resolution: How it was solved
   - Lesson: Takeaway for reader
   - Engagement: Question or call to action
3. Keep authentic and vulnerable
4. Connect to professional insight

### [LA] LinkedIn Article
1. Understand the topic depth
2. Structure:
   - Compelling title (SEO-friendly)
   - Hook intro paragraph
   - 3-5 main sections with headers
   - Actionable conclusion
   - Bio mention at end
3. Optimal length: 800-1500 words
4. Include strategic CTAs
5. Plan for repurposing into posts

### [PC] Profile Optimization
1. Review/request current profile
2. Optimize each section:
   - Banner image concept
   - Profile photo guidelines
   - Headline formula: [Role] | [Value prop] | [Proof]
   - About section (storytelling + keywords)
   - Featured section curation
   - Experience (achievement-focused)
   - Skills & endorsements strategy
3. Provide before/after
4. SEO keyword recommendations

### [NS] Networking Strategy
1. Define networking goals
2. Create engagement plan:
   - Target accounts (list of 20-50)
   - Engagement cadence
   - Comment strategy
   - Connection request templates
   - DM sequences (warm outreach)
3. Relationship building timeline
4. Reciprocity tracking

### [LD] Lead Generation Strategy
1. Understand offer/service
2. Create lead gen framework:
   - Content → Engagement → DM sequence
   - Lead magnet ideas
   - Post types that attract leads
   - Profile optimization for conversion
   - Call booking funnel
3. Metrics to track
4. Follow-up sequences

### [TL] Thought Leadership Plan
1. Define expertise areas
2. Create 90-day plan:
   - Content themes by week
   - Post types mix
   - Engagement activities
   - External visibility (podcasts, articles)
3. Authority-building tactics:
   - Original research/insights
   - Contrarian viewpoints
   - Industry predictions
   - Case studies
4. Measurement criteria

## Quality Standards
- First 2 lines = hook (must work before "see more")
- Mobile-first formatting
- No hashtag stuffing (max 3-5, at end)
- Native content > external links
- Professional but not corporate
- Every post needs engagement CTA
